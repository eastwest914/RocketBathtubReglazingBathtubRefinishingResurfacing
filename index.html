Our Reglazing Process

We arrive at your home and mask the room before any work begins. We strip and sand the surface, repair any chips or cracks, apply a bonding agent and spray our premium professional-grade topcoat in your chosen color. We use only Low-VOC, eco-friendly products. FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about bathtub reglazing, tile refinishing and our services in Delran and Burlington County, NJ.

What is bathtub reglazing?

Bathtub reglazing (also called refinishing or resurfacing) is a process where a technician cleans, repairs and applies a new professional-grade coating to your existing bathtub surface. The result looks and feels brand new at a fraction of the replacement cost. Most jobs are completed the same day in just a few hours and the tub is ready to use within 24 hours.

How much does bathtub reglazing cost vs replacing?

Replacing a bathtub in NJ typically costs $1,500 to $5,000 or more when you factor in the new tub, removal and disposal of the old one, plumbing work and installation. Reglazing is a fraction of that cost. Most NJ homeowners and property managers save 50 to 80 percent by choosing reglazing over replacement — without any construction work or mess.

Do you offer a warranty on your reglazing work?

Yes! Every reglazing and refinishing job we complete in NJ is backed by our industry-leading 5-year warranty. We stand behind every project and our nearly 100 percent customer satisfaction rate is proof of our commitment to quality. Our technician will also provide a full care sheet at the end of each job to help you maintain your new finish.

Can you change the color of my bathtub or tiles?

Yes! One of the great advantages of reglazing is that you can completely change the color of your bathtub, sink or tiles during the refinishing process. Whether you want to modernize an outdated pink or green tub to a crisp white or any other color, our certified technicians can make it happen the same day you call us in Delran or Burlington County, NJ.
